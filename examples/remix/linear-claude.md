# Linear × Claude

## Editorial SaaS, with a soul

Familiar Linear discipline. Claude-warm voice.

---

# What changed

- More whitespace, hairline borders, no shadow
- Body type stays Pretendard — Claude's serif is reserved for storytelling moments
- Bullets become dashes — quieter rhythm
- Orange accent stays — but only on the things that earn it

---

<!-- layout: bento -->

# Three principles

:::card
### Restraint
#### Less ink
The page should look like a finished document, not a control panel.
:::

:::card
### Voice
#### One sentence at a time
Each card is a single idea. Read top-to-bottom, not scan.
:::

:::card
### Trust
#### No fake numbers
Stats are dated and sourced or they aren't on the page.
:::

---

# Where this works

- Internal product strategy decks where leadership reads, not skims
- Series A → B narrative decks; emotional arc matters more than density
- Engineering ADR walkthroughs, slowed down for cross-functional review
