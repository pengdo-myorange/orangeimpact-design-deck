# Three anchors

:::card
### Black
#### Not navy, not charcoal

#### One accent, everywhere
ODS brand orange stays the only highlight. No teal, purple, or rainbow chips.

:::card
### Hard edges
#### 4px max radius
Brutal geometry reads as "engineering", not "dashboard".
:::
