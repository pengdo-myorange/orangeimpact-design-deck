# Playful Color
## Friendly software, serious craft

Figma · Duolingo · Mailchimp — warm hues, round shapes, clear voice.

---

# What changed

- Soft shadows, generous radii (cards 12px, pills 999px)
- Purple as default brand — override per-deck via brand.md accent
- Dot bullets instead of dashes — gentler rhythm
- One hue per section, no rainbow explosions

---

<!-- layout: bento -->

# Three principles

:::card
### Warmth
#### One hue per surface
Pick a hue per section and commit. Don't rainbow-slop the page.
:::

:::card
### Round
#### Pills > rectangles
Buttons and chips use 999px radius. Cards settle at 12px.
:::

:::card
### Flat
#### No neumorphism
Soft 8% shadows — depth, not distortion.
:::

---

# Where this works

- Consumer onboarding and new-user education
- Community-facing product updates and changelog decks
- Brand decks where the product lives alongside illustration
