# Indie Cult
## Cream canvas, warm serifs, terracotta notes

Granola · Criterion · A24 — small-batch software aesthetic.

---

# What changed

- Cream (#faf8f2) canvas — rejects cold neutrals
- Serif headlines (Georgia fallback) at light weights (400–500)
- Terracotta (#b45837) accent — warm brown-orange
- Frosted cards: blur(16px) saturate(150%) on rgba(255,251,244,0.7)
- Warm soft shadows (rgba(180,88,55,0.08))

# Three anchors

:::card
### Cream, not white
#### Reject cold neutrals
#faf8f2 gives paper-warmth at 1920×1080 on projector.
:::

:::card
### Serif headlines
#### Light weight only
Display serifs at 400–500 feel curated, not corporate.
:::

:::card
### Terracotta
#### One warm accent
#b45837 — earthy, like ink on matte card stock.
:::
