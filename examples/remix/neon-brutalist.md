# Neon Brutalist
## Zero radius. Two-pixel rules. Orange, black, white.

The Verge · PlayStation · Bugatti — aggressive geometry, zero shadows.

---

<!-- layout: big-number -->
# 0
Corner radius anywhere in the system. Strict.

---

# What changed

- All radii set to 0 — buttons, cards, chips, images
- 2px solid black borders as universal separators
- Orange (#ff6600) as the only accent — black and white do the rest
- Shadows removed entirely — depth from size and inversion

---

<!-- layout: bento -->

# Three rules

:::card
### No radius
#### Rectangles only
No pills. No rounded buttons. No softened chips.
:::

:::card
### No shadows
#### Rules instead
2px black separators carry the whole hierarchy.
:::

:::card
### Orange + ink
#### One accent
Orange on black. Black on white. Inversion, not gradient.
:::

---

# Where this works

- Editorial product announcements with an opinion
- Launch pages for hardware that wants to feel industrial
- Engineering-team decks that reject "dashboard soft" aesthetics
